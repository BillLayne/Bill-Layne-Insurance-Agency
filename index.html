Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How does an independent agent save me money?

Unlike captive agents who work for one company, we partner with over 10 top-rated carriers. We do the shopping for you, comparing policies and prices to find the perfect fit.

What do I need to get a quote?

For auto: driver's license, vehicle info, and driving history. For home: property address and basic home details. The whole process takes about 15 minutes!

Can you help me switch insurers?

Absolutely! We make switching easy. Once we find you a better policy, we'll handle all the paperwork to ensure there are no gaps in coverage.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve all of North Carolina, with a focus on Surry, Wilkes, and Yadkin counties. Our main office is in Elkin.

How often should I review my coverage?

We recommend reviewing annually or after major life changes. We'll send reminders and help ensure your coverage keeps pace with your life.

What if I have a claim?

We're here to help! We'll guide you through the process, work with the insurance company on your behalf, and ensure you get a fair settlement.
