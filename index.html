Absolutely not! Getting a quote is 100% free with zero obligation. We'll never pressure you to buy. Our job is to present your options and let you decide what's best for your family.

Can you help me switch from my current insurer?

Yes! We handle all the paperwork and coordinate the transition to ensure there's no gap in coverage. It's completely hassle-free on your end.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve all of North Carolina with expertise in Surry, Wilkes, and Yadkin counties. Our main office is located in Elkin on North Bridge Street.

What if I need to file a claim?

We're here to help! Call us immediately and we'll guide you through the entire claims process. We work as your advocate to ensure you get a fair and fast settlement.

What are the minimum car insurance requirements in North Carolina?

North Carolina requires minimum liability coverage of 50/100/50: $50,000 bodily injury per person, $100,000 bodily injury per accident, and $50,000 property damage. However, we recommend higher limits to better protect your assets. Call us at (336) 835-1993 for a free coverage review.

How much does home insurance cost in Surry County NC?

Average home insurance in Surry County ranges from $800 to $1,500 annually, depending on your home's value, age, and coverage level. Our customers in Elkin, Mount Airy, and surrounding areas often save 20-40% by comparing multiple carriers. Get your free quote today to see your actual cost.

Do you serve Mount Airy and other cities outside Elkin?

Yes! We serve all of Surry County including Mount Airy, Jonesville, Dobson, and Pilot Mountain. We also serve Wilkes County (Wilkesboro, North Wilkesboro), Yadkin County (Yadkinville), and all of North Carolina. Local service with statewide reach.

Can you help me get SR-22 insurance in North Carolina?

Absolutely! We specialize in SR-22 and high-risk insurance for drivers in Elkin and throughout North Carolina. We work with multiple carriers who offer SR-22 filing, often at competitive rates. Call (336) 835-1993 for immediate assistance - we can often get you covered the same day.
