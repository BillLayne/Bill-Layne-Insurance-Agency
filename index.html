North Carolina does not use SR-22 forms. Instead, NC uses Form DL-123 — a certificate proving you carry at least the state minimum liability insurance (50/100/50). It's required for license reinstatement after DWI, driving without insurance, or suspension. Unlike SR-22 in other states, the DL-123 is valid for only 30 days — you must visit the DMV before it expires. We handle same-day DL-123 filings. Call (336) 835-1993.
A captive agent (like State Farm or Allstate agents) can only sell policies from one company. An independent agent like Bill Layne Insurance represents multiple carriers such as Progressive, Travelers, and Nationwide, allowing us to compare rates and coverage options across companies to find the best fit for your needs and budget. You get more choices and we work for you, not the insurance company.
While not legally required in NC, renters insurance is highly recommended. Your landlord's policy does NOT cover your personal belongings if there's a fire, theft, or water damage. Renters insurance also provides liability coverage if someone is injured in your apartment. Policies typically start around $15-$25 per month and can be bundled with your auto insurance for potential additional savings.
We recommend reviewing your insurance at least once a year or whenever you experience a major life change such as buying a new home, adding a teen driver, getting married, or starting a business. In North Carolina, driving without insurance is a Class 1 misdemeanor. If your coverage lapses, the NC DMV can revoke your registration and license plates. You may also face higher premiums when you reinstate coverage because carriers view a lapse as a risk factor. The Safe Driver Incentive Plan (SDIP) is North Carolina's unique points system that directly affects your insurance premiums. Points are assigned for at-fault accidents and moving violations and remain on your record for 3 years. Even one speeding ticket can increase your rate. We can review your SDIP record and shop carriers that offer the most competitive rates for your point level. Call (336) 835-1993 for a free review.
