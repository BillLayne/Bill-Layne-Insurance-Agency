Quick answers to the most common questions from NC drivers and homeowners.

How much can I save on car insurance in North Carolina?
As an independent agency, Bill Layne Insurance compares rates from 7 carriers including Travelers, Progressive, and NC Grange to help find you a competitive rate. Your savings will depend on your driving record, vehicle, and coverage needs. Our customers save an average of $847/year.
Do you offer SR-22 insurance in Elkin NC?
Yes, we specialize in SR-22 filings and high-risk coverage for all North Carolina drivers. We can often get you covered the same day and file the SR-22 with the NC DMV immediately. Call us at (336) 835-1993 to get started.
What is the best way to bundle auto and home insurance in NC?
Bundling auto and home insurance is one of the easiest ways to save. Many of our carriers offer multi-policy discounts, typically ranging from 5–25%, when you combine auto and home policies. We'll compare bundle rates across all our carriers to maximize your savings.
What should I do if I have an insurance claim in North Carolina?
Call us first at (336) 835-1993! As your advocate, we can advise you on the claims process. We'll help you file the claim with the carrier and ensure it's handled fairly and promptly. That's the advantage of having a local agent in Elkin.
What is the difference between an independent agent and a captive agent?
A captive agent (like State Farm or Allstate agents) can only sell policies from one company. An independent agent like Bill Layne Insurance represents multiple carriers such as Progressive, Travelers, and Nationwide, allowing us to compare rates and coverage options to find the best fit for your needs. You get more choices and we work for you, not the insurance company.
What is the NC Safe Driver Incentive Plan (SDIP)?
The Safe Driver Incentive Plan (SDIP) is North Carolina's unique points system that directly affects your insurance premiums. Points are assigned for at-fault accidents and moving violations and remain on your record for 3 years. We can review your SDIP record and shop carriers that offer the most competitive rates for your point level. Call (336) 835-1993 for a free review.
