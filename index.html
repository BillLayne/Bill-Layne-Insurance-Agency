Frequently Asked Questions

How does an independent agent save me money?

Unlike captive agents who work for one company, we partner with over 10 top-rated insurance carriers. We do the shopping for you, comparing policies and prices to find the perfect fit for your needs and budget.

What do I need to get a quote?

For an auto quote, you'll need your driver's license, vehicle information, and driving history. For home insurance, we'll need your property address and basic home details. The whole process takes about 15 minutes!

Can you help me switch from my current insurer?

Absolutely! We make switching easy. Once we find you a better policy, we'll help with all the paperwork to ensure there are no gaps in coverage. Our goal is a seamless transition.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve all of North Carolina, with a focus on Surry, Wilkes, and Yadkin counties. Our main office is in Elkin, but we work with clients throughout the state.

How often should I review my coverage?

We recommend reviewing your insurance annually or after major life changes like buying a home, getting married, or having children. We'll send you reminders and help ensure your coverage keeps pace with your life.
