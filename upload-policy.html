Quick Questions

What if I don’t have my policy file?

Easiest option: grab the paper copy and snap a photo with your phone — even just the cover page is enough to get started. Or submit the form without a file and we’ll email instructions to download a copy from your carrier’s website (about 30 seconds).

Will you bug me with phone calls?

No. Our review comes back by email. We only call if you ask us to or if there’s a fast-acting issue (like an upcoming renewal we can save you on). One human, one email — that’s the deal.

Do I have to switch?

Never. Sometimes the honest answer is “your current rate is already great — stay where you are.” If we can save you money, we’ll show the math and let you decide. No pressure, no hard sell.

How is my policy file kept private?

Your file goes directly to Bill’s secure agency inbox. It’s reviewed only by Bill or his licensed team. We don’t share, sell, or post your data anywhere. We’re a small NC agency, not a lead-aggregator service.
