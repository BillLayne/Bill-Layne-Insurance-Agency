Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our insurance services

How quickly can I get a quote?
We can typically provide you with a comprehensive insurance quote within 15-30 minutes. For auto insurance, we can often quote you while you're on the phone. For home or commercial insurance, we may need a bit more information, but we'll work quickly to get you competitive rates from multiple carriers. Just call us at (336) 835-1993 or fill out our contact form to get started.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes! We understand that paying for insurance all at once can be challenging. Most of our insurance carriers offer flexible payment plans, including monthly payment options. We'll help you find a payment schedule that fits your budget while ensuring you maintain continuous coverage. Ask about payment options when you get your quote.
Can you help with claims from other carriers?
While we primarily assist with claims for policies we've written, we're always happy to provide guidance and advice to anyone in our community. If you're having trouble with a claim from another carrier, give us a call. We can often provide helpful insights about the claims process and may be able to help you understand your options. Plus, this might be a good time to review your coverage and see if we can offer you better protection and service.
What insurance companies do you represent?
As an independent insurance agency, we represent multiple top-rated insurance carriers. This allows us to shop your coverage with several companies to find the best combination of price and protection for your specific needs. Our carriers include both national brands and strong regional companies that specialize in North Carolina coverage. Contact us to learn which carriers would be the best fit for your insurance needs.
