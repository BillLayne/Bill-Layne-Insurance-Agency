Frequently Asked Questions - King NC Insurance

What types of insurance do you offer in King NC?

We offer auto, home, business, life, motorcycle, and SR-22 insurance throughout King NC 27021 and Stokes County. As an independent agency, we compare rates from 9 carriers to find you the best deal.

Do you serve commuters from King to Winston-Salem?

Yes! Many King residents commute to Winston-Salem via Hwy 52. We help find the best auto insurance rates for daily commuters, including multi-car discounts and safe driver savings.

Is there a local office near King?

Our Elkin office at 1283 N Bridge St is about 30 minutes from King via Hwy 268. We also have a Dobson office at 209 S Main St, about 25 minutes away. Call (336) 835-1993 for same-day quotes.

Can you insure rural properties and farms near King?

Absolutely. We specialize in insuring rural properties throughout Stokes County, including homes on acreage, farms, outbuildings, and properties near Hanging Rock and Pilot Mountain.
