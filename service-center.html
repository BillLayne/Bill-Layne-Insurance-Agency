Frequently Asked Questions

Call us at (336) 835-1993 during business hours and we'll help you file your claim. After hours, contact your carrier's 24/7 claims line found on your insurance ID card or use the "Report a Claim" section above, then call us the next business day.

You can pay your insurance bill directly through your carrier's online portal. Use the "Pay Your Bill" section above to find your carrier's payment link. You can also contact our office at (336) 835-1993 and we'll help you find the right payment option.

Contact us at (336) 835-1993 or email Save@BillLayneInsurance.com with your policy number and the changes you need. Common changes include adding vehicles, updating addresses, changing coverage limits, and adding or removing drivers. We handle all carrier communication for you.

Call us at (336) 835-1993 or email Save@BillLayneInsurance.com and we'll send you a digital copy of your insurance ID card right away. Most carriers also offer mobile apps where you can access your ID card digitally at any time.
