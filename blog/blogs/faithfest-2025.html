Frequently Asked Questions

What is the schedule for FaithFest 2025 in Wilkesboro?

Friday starts at 3:00 PM with Caleb & John, ending with Zach Williams and fireworks. Saturday begins at 12:00 PM with Leanna Crawford, closing with Phil Wickham.

How much are tickets for FaithFest 2025?

Tickets range from $55-$75 for single-day or two-day passes, available online at FaithFestNC.com.

Is FaithFest family-friendly?

Yes, FaithFest offers activities for kids, food vendors, and a welcoming environment for all ages.

What should I bring to FaithFest 2025?

Bring a lawn chair, sunscreen, and water; no chairs are provided on-site.
