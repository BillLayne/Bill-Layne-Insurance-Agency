Wineries & Vineyards: Wine Country Romance

Surry County sits at the heart of the Yadkin Valley AVA (American Viticultural Area), North Carolina's first federally recognized wine region. With over 40 wineries within a short drive, you'll find the perfect backdrop for romance among rolling vineyards and mountain vistas.

JOLO Winery & Vineyards

Recognized as one of the Top 100 Most Romantic Restaurants in America by OpenTable, JOLO offers an unparalleled dining experience at their EndPosts Restaurant. The venue provides breathtaking views of Pilot Mountain, especially stunning during sunset.

📍 219 Jolo Winery Ln, Pilot Mountain ⏰ Thurs-Sun (Reservations Required) 💰 $$$-$$$$ (Worth the splurge) Year-round

Insider Tip: Book a table on the patio 90 minutes before sunset for the most romantic experience. The wine list features their award-winning Jolotage, perfect for sharing. Note: This is a 21+ only venue, ensuring a sophisticated atmosphere.

Shelton Vineyards

North Carolina's largest family-owned estate winery offers multiple romantic experiences. Tour the pristine grounds, enjoy a guided tasting, then dine at the elegant Harvest Grill overlooking the vineyards.

📍 286 Cabernet Lane, Dobson ⏰ Mon-Sun 11am-5pm (Restaurant hours vary) 💰 $$-$$$ (Tastings from $18) Best: Spring/Fall

Perfect Date: Start with the winery tour at 2pm, followed by a tasting of their Bourbon Barrel Chardonnay. Reserve dinner at Harvest Grill for 5:30pm - the Sixty South Salmon is exceptional. For overnight romance, the Hampton Inn at Shelton Vineyards is the only hotel chain location with its own wine bar!

Adagio Vineyards

Where music and wine create perfect harmony. Owners Jan and Tim Wahl combine their passions - she's a violinist and violin maker, he's the winemaker. You might be serenaded during your tasting!

📍 139 Benge Drive, Elkin ⏰ Thurs-Sat 12-5pm, Sun 1-5pm 💰 $$ (Tastings $16, Flights $22) Live music weekends

Romance Factor: The guided tastings are intimate and educational, lasting 40+ minutes. Sip wine by the fire pit or on the porch. Their semi-sweet "Coda" is perfect for couples who can't agree on dry vs. sweet wines. Pet-friendly if you want to bring your "fur baby" along!

Outdoor Adventures: Love in Nature

For couples who believe the best dates involve fresh air and stunning views, Surry County's natural beauty provides endless romantic possibilities, from easy strolls to adventurous hikes.

Pilot Mountain State Park

Rising 1,400 feet above the surrounding countryside, this iconic peak offers some of the most romantic sunset views in North Carolina. The distinctive rocky pinnacle has served as a landmark for centuries.

📍 1792 Pilot Knob Park Rd, Pinnacle ⏰ 7am-sunset daily 💰 Free (State Park) Best: Fall & Spring

Romantic Spots: Little Pinnacle Overlook offers moderate hiking with spectacular views. For an easy option, drive to the summit parking area and walk the short Jomeokee Trail. Pack a picnic and arrive 2 hours before sunset. The fall foliage (October) is absolutely breathtaking!

Hidden Gem: The unnamed first overlook provides Half Dome-like views with fewer crowds.

Blue Ridge Parkway Overlooks

Just 10 minutes from Mount Airy, access points at Mileposts 199.4, 217.3, and 229.7 lead to romantic vistas. Cumberland Knob (MP 217.5) offers easy trails perfect for sunset strolls.

📍 Multiple access points from Surry County ⏰ 24/7 (Best at sunrise/sunset) 💰 Free Peak: October

Date Idea: Drive to Thunder Hill Overlook (MP 290.3) for stargazing. Bring blankets, hot cocoa in a thermos, and download a stargazing app. The 180° panorama is perfect for watching meteor showers or just counting stars together.

Romantic Restaurants: Culinary Connections

From intimate bistros to upscale dining rooms, Surry County's restaurant scene offers the perfect ambiance for romantic dinners, whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just because.

Southern on Main

Elkin's premier dining destination combines Southern comfort with upscale flair. This family-owned gem on Main Street is where locals go to celebrate special moments.

📍 102 E Main St, Elkin ⏰ Lunch & Dinner (Closed Mon-Tues) 💰 $$-$$$ Outdoor seating Apr-Oct

Must Try: Their rotating seasonal menu always impresses, but the Angus filet and fresh seafood specials are consistently outstanding. Make reservations for outdoor seating - the twinkling lights create magic. Pair dinner with cocktails at nearby Fruition bar before or after.

Harvest Grill at Shelton Vineyards

Upscale dining in a bistro atmosphere with vineyard views. The enclosed patio with stone fireplace creates an intimate setting year-round.

📍 286 Cabernet Lane, Dobson ⏰ Wed-Sun Dinner, Sat-Sun Lunch 💰 $$$ Fireplace cozy in winter

Seasonal Romance: Year-Round Date Ideas

Spring (March-May)

  • Budbreak Wine Festival: Celebrate new wine releases at participating wineries
  • Pilot Mountain Wildflowers: Hike among blooming mountain laurel and rhododendron
  • Picnic at Round Peak Vineyards: Disc golf and wine with mountain views

Summer (June-August)

  • Blackmon Amphitheatre Concerts: Dance under the stars in downtown Mount Airy
  • Yadkin River Float: Lazy river tubing or kayaking adventures
  • Sunset at Serre Vineyards: Friday evening wine with food trucks

Fall (September-November)

  • Autumn Leaves Festival: October festival with music and local crafts
  • Vineyard Harvest Tours: See grape picking and crushing in action
  • Blue Ridge Parkway Drive: Peak foliage typically mid-October

Winter (December-February)

  • Yuletide Sippin' Tour: Visit 9 wineries, collect ornaments (Nov-Dec)
  • Cozy Fireside Dining: Harvest Grill's stone fireplace ambiance
  • Historic Elkin Hotel: Book a suite at Three Trails Hotel

Budget-Friendly Romance
