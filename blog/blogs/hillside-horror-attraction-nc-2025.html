Discover the Spine-Chilling Secrets of Hillside Horror in Roaring River, NC – Your Ultimate Guide to Heart-Pounding Halloween Thrills!

Just a short drive from Elkin, an award-winning nightmare awaits. We're pulling back the curtain on what makes Hillside Horror the most talked-about haunt in North Carolina.

By Bill Layne Insurance Team | Elkin, NC | Published September 8, 2025

As the leaves turn and a chill fills the North Carolina air, thrill-seekers across the state ask one question: where can we find the best scares? For those of us in the Elkin and Roaring River area, the answer is right in our backyard. We're talking about Hillside Horror, an attraction that has earned a reputation for being one of the most intense and terrifying haunts in the entire state.

But what makes it so scary? Is it worth the hype? As your local community experts, we've gathered all the intel you need to survive a night at this legendary haunted attraction.

A Walk-Through of Your Worst Nightmares 👻

Hillside Horror isn't just a quick walk-through. It’s a multi-part journey designed to attack your senses and prey on your deepest fears. Voted the #1 Haunted Attraction in North Carolina by TheScareFactor.com, it combines a terrifying wooded trail, a haunted corn maze, a disturbing old barn, and a final, twisted house into one seamless nightmare.

The Shadowed Woods & Corn Maze

Your journey begins on a long, winding trail through pitch-black woods where you feel completely isolated. The sound design is incredible; every snap of a twig or distant scream feels real. Visitors often say the corn maze is the most disorienting part. The narrow paths and high stalks create a claustrophobic feeling, and you never know what's waiting around the next corner.

The Gruesome Old Barn & The House

The actors in the barn and the final house are relentless. Their energy is what makes this haunt so memorable. The sets are movie-quality, filled with gruesome details that are hard to forget. Feedback consistently praises the intensity here—it's not just jump scares, but a deeply unsettling atmosphere that stays with you long after you leave.

What Visitors Are Screaming About:

  • Incredible Value: This is a long trail. Most people spend 30-40 minutes getting through, making it one of the best bangs for your buck in NC.
  • Passionate Actors: You can tell the scare actors love what they do. They are energetic, intimidating, and stay in character.
  • True Terror: This is a genuinely scary haunt. It's designed for thrill-seekers and is not recommended for young children or the faint of heart.
