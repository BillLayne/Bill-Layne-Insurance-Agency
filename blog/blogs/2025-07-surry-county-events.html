Your Weekend Adventure Blueprint

  • Plan for 3 Days: Dedicate one day each to hiking, paddling, and wine tasting for a relaxed pace.
  • Book Ahead: Reserve kayaks and winery tours in advance, especially during peak season (spring and fall).
  • Hike Early: Start your hike at Pilot Mountain early in the morning to avoid crowds and afternoon heat.
  • Check River Levels: Always check the Yadkin River water levels and flow rates before your paddling trip.
  • Stay Protected: A fun weekend is a safe weekend. Ensure your gear and plans are covered.

What is the best day hike in Surry County?

The best day hike in Surry County is the Pilot Mountain State Park Grindstone and Ledge Spring Trail loop. This moderately challenging 3-mile loop offers the most diverse scenery, including close-up views of the iconic Big Pinnacle, breathtaking long-range vistas, and a unique forested trail experience, making it the most rewarding hike for most visitors.

Your weekend kicks off with a true North Carolina icon: Pilot Mountain. While you can drive to the top for a quick look, the real magic happens on the trails. The Grindstone and Ledge Spring Trail loop gives you the full experience. You'll start in the lush forest, climb alongside impressive rock ledges, and be rewarded with panoramic views that stretch for miles across the Piedmont.

Your Hiking Plan:

  • Activity: Hiking the Grindstone & Ledge Spring Trail Loop
  • Location: Pilot Mountain State Park
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate (some steep sections and stairs)
  • Pro-Tip: Pack more water than you think you need and wear sturdy shoes with good grip. The views from the top are the perfect spot for a pre-packed lunch.

Where can I paddle on the Yadkin River?

You can easily paddle on the Yadkin River by starting at the Shoals Access point near Pilot Mountain and floating downstream to the Hauser Town Road Access. This popular 6-mile stretch is perfect for beginners, offering calm waters, beautiful scenery, and a relaxing 3-to-4-hour trip serviced by local outfitters in the area.

After yesterday's hike, it's time to cool off and see Surry County from a different perspective. The Yadkin River Blueway offers miles of gentle, scenic paddling perfect for kayaks or canoes. A trip from Shoals Access is a local favorite, taking you through quiet forests and past rolling farmland. Keep an eye out for herons, deer, and other wildlife along the banks.

Your Paddling Plan:

  • Activity: Kayaking or Canoeing
  • Location: Yadkin River, from Shoals Access
  • Outfitters: Rocky Forest River Run or Yadkin River Adventures
  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Pro-Tip: Book your rental and shuttle service with a local outfitter. They make it easy by dropping you off upstream and picking you up at the end point. Don't forget a waterproof bag for your phone and keys!

Which wineries should I visit in the Yadkin Valley?

For a great introduction to the Yadkin Valley, you should visit a trio of wineries like JOLO Winery & Vineyards for stunning views and premium wines, Shelton Vineyards for its grand scale and comprehensive tours, and Round Peak Vineyards for a relaxed, dog-friendly atmosphere. This combination offers a diverse and high-quality tasting experience.

No trip to Surry County is complete without experiencing the Yadkin Valley wine region. After two days of adventure, your final day is for relaxing and savoring the local flavor. With dozens of wineries to choose from, you can't go wrong, but visiting a few with different styles gives you the best taste of what the area has to offer.

Your Adventure Weekend FAQs

When is the best time of year to visit? +

The best times for this itinerary are Spring (April-May) and Fall (September-October). The weather is mild, the spring flowers or fall colors are beautiful, and you'll avoid the summer humidity. However, the area is accessible and enjoyable year-round.

Is this weekend itinerary family-friendly? +

Absolutely! The hiking and paddling are great for older children and teens. Many wineries are family-friendly, offering grounds to explore and grape juice for the kids. Always check a specific winery's policy before you visit.

Do I need my own kayak or gear? +

Not at all. Local outfitters like Rocky Forest River Run provide everything you need: kayaks or canoes, paddles, life vests, and shuttle service. Just show up ready for fun.

What should I do if the weather is bad? +

Surry County has great indoor options! You can explore the Andy Griffith Museum in Mount Airy, visit the historic Rockford General Store, or spend more time tasting indoors at the many wineries. A rainy day is a perfect excuse to focus on the "Sip" part of your trip!
