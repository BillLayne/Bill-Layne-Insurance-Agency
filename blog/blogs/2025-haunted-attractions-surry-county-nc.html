NC's Premier Haunts: The Legends of Fear

Kersey Valley Spooky Woods

1615 Kersey Valley Rd, Archdale, NC 27263

Scream Park
Iconic

Prepare for a full-scale assault on your senses. Spooky Woods is a Halloween institution that has been perfecting the art of the scare for over four decades. Your journey begins on a tram that takes you deep into the darkness, dropping you off to face a gauntlet of terror. You’ll navigate incredibly detailed, Hollywood-level sets, from eerie mansions to haunted forests filled with iconic horror villains. It’s more than a haunted house; it’s an immersive Halloween event designed for true horror aficionados.

Lake Hickory Haunts

520 Carolina Ave, Hickory, NC 28601

Waterfront
Immersive

What happens when you mix high-tech scares with the primal fear of dark water? You get Lake Hickory Haunts. This one-of-a-kind attraction uses its lakeside setting to amplify the terror. With 13 interconnected attractions, you’ll seamlessly transition from the industrial nightmare of the `Nightmare Factory` to a cursed pirate ship on the water's edge. The multi-dimensional special effects, combined with the eerie reflections on the lake, create a uniquely disorienting and terrifying experience you won’t find anywhere else.

Woods of Terror

5601 N Church St, Greensboro, NC 27455

Haunted Trail
Variety

If you’re the kind of person who can't pick just one favorite horror movie, this is your haunt. Woods of Terror is a sprawling horror theme park that throws everything at you. One ticket gives you access to a massive trail featuring over 16 unique attractions. One moment you're navigating a disorienting 3D clown maze, the next you're trying to escape a spider-infested cavern in `Arachnophobia`. In between scares, the massive Monster Midway offers carnival games and food, creating a festival atmosphere dedicated to fear.

Haunted Attractions in Surry County, NC

Looking for scares closer to home? You don't have to travel far for top-tier frights. Surry County offers a more intimate and historical brand of fear, with fantastic haunted houses and ghost tours just a short drive from Elkin and Pilot Mountain.

Historic Mount Airy Ghost Tours

301 N Main St, Mt Airy, NC 27030

Ghost Tour
Family Friendly

Step away from the jump scares and into the chilling embrace of history. This lantern-lit walking tour explores the documented hauntings and strange legends of downtown Mount Airy. You'll stand in the exact spots where ghostly apparitions have been seen and hear true tales of love, loss, and tragedy that have left a supernatural imprint on the town. It’s a captivating experience for history buffs and paranormal enthusiasts alike, offering a different kind of Halloween fright.

Visitor Feedback:

"A fantastic way to learn about the town's history with a spooky twist. The stories are fascinating and our guide was a great storyteller."

The Haunting at Miss Angels Farm

252 Heart Lane, Mt Airy, NC 27030

Haunted Corn Maze
Local Favorite

This is authentic, grassroots horror at its finest. Forget high-tech animatronics; Miss Angels Farm relies on the primal fear of what lurks in the darkness. The main attraction is the haunted corn maze, where the rustling stalks and shadowy figures create a constant sense of dread. You're isolated, you're lost, and you're not alone. It's a classic, old-school haunt that proves you don’t need a massive budget to be truly terrifying.

Hillside Horror

2311 NC-268, Roaring River, NC 28669

Haunted Woods
High-Intensity

If your ideal haunted house leaves you breathless and questioning your sanity, Hillside Horror is your destination. This is not a casual stroll through the woods; it’s a grueling, high-intensity endurance test of fear. The trail is notoriously long, designed to wear you down before the biggest scares even hit. Actors are permitted to get up close and personal, and the scenes are designed to be as graphic and unsettling as possible. This is a raw, aggressive haunt for serious thrill-seekers only.
