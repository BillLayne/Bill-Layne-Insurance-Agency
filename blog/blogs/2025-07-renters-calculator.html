Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a calculator for renters insurance?+

Most renters significantly underestimate the value of their belongings. A calculator prevents you from being underinsured. If a disaster like a fire or theft occurs, you'll have an accurate inventory and valuation to ensure your insurance claim can cover the full replacement cost of your items.

What's replacement costThe amount it would cost to replace your damaged property with new items of similar kind and quality, without deducting for depreciation. vs. actual cash valueThe replacement cost of your property minus depreciation. This means older items are worth less than newer ones.?+

Replacement Cost Value (RCV) is the cost to replace your damaged item with a new, similar item. Actual Cash Value (ACV) is the replacement cost minus depreciation. We almost always recommend RCV coverage for our clients, as it provides much better protection.

Are expensive items like jewelry or art covered?+

Standard renters policies have sub-limits for high-value items like jewelry, firearms, and fine art. During our quote process, we'll ask about these items to ensure they are properly covered, often with a special endorsement called a "floater" or "rider".

Is my data from this calculator saved?+

No. This calculator operates entirely within your browser for your privacy and security. We do not see or store any of the data you input unless you choose to share it with us when requesting a quote.
