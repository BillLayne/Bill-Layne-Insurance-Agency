Does Steadily cover flooding in NC?
Standard Steadily policies, like most in NC, exclude surface water flooding. However, at Bill Layne Insurance, we can pair your Steadily policy with a private flood rider to ensure 100% protection in low-lying Surry County areas.
What is a DP-3 form?
A DP-3 (Dwelling Fire Form 3) is an 'Open Perils' policy. It covers everything except what is explicitly excluded. This is the gold standard for North Carolina landlords who want comprehensive structural protection.
Can I switch to Steadily mid-term?
Absolutely. We handle the mortgage notification and the cancellation of your old policy. Many local landlords switch mid-term just to lock in lower 2026 price tiers before the June hike.
