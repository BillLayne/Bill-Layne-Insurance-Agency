A Rich History, Rooted in North Carolina

NC Grange Mutual isn't a giant, faceless corporation from another state. They were founded right here in North Carolina way back in 1934. Think about that for a second! They've seen our state through hurricanes, ice storms, and everything in between. Their whole purpose was to help North Carolina's communities, especially farmers and folks in rural areas, get fair and honest insurance.

They are a "mutual" insurance company, which means they are owned by their policyholders—people like you! They don't have to answer to stockholders on Wall Street. Their only job is to serve you, their members. That's a big difference.

Financial Strength & Competitive Rates

It's important to know that the company protecting your home is financially solid. You want to be sure they can pay your claim if something bad happens. NC Grange Mutual is rated by Demotech, Inc., a company that specializes in analyzing the financial health of insurance companies. This rating shows they have the financial strength to be there when you need them most.

Being a mutual company also helps. Any profits they make go right back into the company to keep it strong for policyholders, not into the pockets of distant investors. This helps keep rates fair and ensures they're ready for the next big storm.

Here’s a key advantage: NC Grange Mutual does not "consent to rate" in North Carolina. In simple terms, this means their rates are closely regulated by the state, making them very competitive. Unlike larger carriers that can charge higher, deviated rates, NC Grange sticks to the approved rates, often saving you money.

Coverage That Fits Your North Carolina Life

While they are experts in homeowners insurance, NC Grange Mutual understands that life in our state is diverse. That's why they offer specialized coverage designed for how North Carolinians live.

Homeowners Insurance

This is their cornerstone. They provide comprehensive protection for your dwelling, personal property, and liability, with policies that are tailored to the specific needs of NC homeowners.

Farm Insurance

Staying true to their roots, they offer some of the best farm coverage in the state. This protects everything from your farmhouse and barns to your equipment and livestock.

Flexible Solutions for Unique Properties

Do you have a trampoline, a pool, a pond, or a specific dog breed? Many big insurance companies will simply refuse to cover your home because of these. NC Grange Mutual takes a different, common-sense approach. For many "hard-to-place" situations, they can offer a policy by simply excluding liability for that specific item.

This means you can still get excellent protection for your house, outbuildings, and belongings, even if you have things like:

  • Specific dog breeds
  • Horses or other livestock
  • Trampolines, pools, or ponds
  • ATVs or hunting activities on your property
  • Other special liability situations

This flexibility ensures more North Carolina families can get the coverage they need without being unfairly penalized.
