When is MerleFest 2026 and how do I get there from Elkin or Mount Airy?

MerleFest 2026 runs April 23-26 at Wilkes Community College in Wilkesboro, NC. From Elkin, take US-21 South to I-77 South, then NC-268 West — about 30 minutes. Does my NC auto insurance cover me driving to festivals and events?

Yes, your North Carolina auto insurance travels with your vehicle wherever you drive in the state, including to festivals and events. However, more miles and heavier traffic increase your risk. Make sure your policy meets the new 50/100/50 minimum limits now required on all NC policies renewed on or after July 1, 2025, and consider whether your collision and comprehensive deductibles are set where you can afford them.

What are the new NC auto insurance minimum limits for 2026?

North Carolina now requires 50/100/50 minimum liability limits — that is $50,000 per person, $100,000 per accident for bodily injury, and $50,000 for property damage — plus mandatory UM/UIM coverage on every policy renewed on or after July 1, 2025. This is a significant increase from the old 30/60/25 minimums and means most NC drivers are carrying more coverage than ever before.
