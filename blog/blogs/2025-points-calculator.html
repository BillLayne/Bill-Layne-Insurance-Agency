Why One Ticket Gives You Two Kinds of Points

In North Carolina, a ticket gives you points in two different systems. It's confusing, but the "money points" are usually worse than the "license points."

License Points vs. Money Points

DMV "License" Points

Think of these like a report card for your driving. Get too many, and the DMV might suspend your license. They're about whether you get to keep driving.

Insurance "Money" Points

These points make your insurance price go up by a set amount for 3 years (or more soon!). They are all about how much you have to pay.

Why Holiday Driving is Extra Risky

On holiday weekends like Labor Day, there are way more police on the roads. They are looking for drunk drivers, but they end up giving out tons of other tickets, too.
