Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How quickly can I get a quote?
We can typically provide you with a comprehensive insurance quote within 15-30 minutes. For auto insurance, we can often quote you while you're on the phone. For home or commercial insurance, we may need a bit more information, but we'll work quickly to get you competitive rates from multiple carriers.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes! We understand that paying for insurance all at once can be challenging. Most of our insurance carriers offer flexible payment plans, including monthly payment options. We'll help you find a payment schedule that fits your budget while ensuring you maintain continuous coverage.
Can you help with claims from other carriers?
While we primarily assist with claims for policies we've written, we're always happy to provide guidance and advice to anyone in our community. If you're having trouble with a claim from another carrier, give us a call. We can often provide helpful insights about the claims process.
What insurance companies do you represent?
As an independent insurance agency, we represent multiple top-rated insurance carriers. This allows us to shop your coverage with several companies to find the best combination of price and protection for your specific needs. Our carriers include both national brands and strong regional companies that specialize in North Carolina coverage.
