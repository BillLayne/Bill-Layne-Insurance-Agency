Explore Elkin

Culture & Trails

The Ultimate Trail Town

Nestled in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Elkin has earned its reputation as North Carolina's premier trail town. Three major trails converge here -- the Mountains-to-Sea Trail, the Overmountain Victory National Historic Trail, and the Yadkin River Blue Water Trail -- making it a haven for hikers, paddlers, and outdoor enthusiasts of every kind.

But Elkin is far more than trailheads and mountain views. Downtown has experienced a remarkable revitalization, with the historic Reeves Theater anchoring a vibrant arts and entertainment scene. Angry Troll Brewing and Elkin Railyard pour craft favorites, while local art galleries and boutiques line the charming streets. It is a place where small-town warmth meets creative energy.

Downtown Highlights

  • Live music and shows at the historic Reeves Theater
  • Craft beer at Elkin Railyard and Angry Troll Brewing
  • Heritage & Trails Center for maps and local history
  • Downtown Mural Hike -- public art around every corner
  • Yadkin Valley Vineyards just minutes away

Quick Facts

  • Location: Surry County, NC
    Gateway to the Yadkin Valley
  • Vibe: Outdoorsy, Artistic, Vibrant, Historic
