About ACORD Forms

ACORD forms are copyrighted industry-standard documents used by insurance professionals nationwide. Contact our office to request completed copies of any ACORD form — we'll prepare them with your policy information included.

ACORD Industry Forms

Standard insurance documents used nationwide

ACORD 25

Certificate of Liability Insurance

Proof of auto or general liability coverage. Often required by lenders, landlords, or business partners.

Download PDF Request Completed
ACORD 27

Evidence of Property Insurance

Proof of homeowners or dwelling coverage. Required by mortgage companies and lenders at closing or renewal.

Download PDF Request Completed
ACORD 28

Evidence of Commercial Property

Proof of commercial property coverage for business owners. Required by landlords, lenders, and lease agreements.

Download PDF Request Completed
ACORD 35

Cancellation Request / Policy Release

Request to cancel an existing insurance policy or release it to another agent. Includes effective date and reason.

Download PDF Online Form Email
ACORD 36

Policy Change Request

Request changes to an existing policy — add/remove vehicles, update addresses, change coverage limits, add drivers.

Download PDF Request Change
ACORD 45

Loss Notice / First Report

Report a new claim or loss to your insurance company. Used for auto accidents, property damage, or liability incidents.

Download PDF Online Form Email
ACORD 60

Dwelling Loss Notice

Report damage to your home or dwelling. Covers fire, wind, water damage, theft, and other property losses.

Download PDF Report Loss
ACORD 125

Commercial Insurance Application

Apply for commercial business insurance. Covers general business info, locations, operations, and coverage needs.

Download PDF Request Help
ACORD 126

Commercial General Liability

Supplement to the commercial application. Details your CGL coverage needs, operations, and liability exposures.

Download PDF Request Help
ACORD 130

Workers Compensation Application

Apply for workers comp coverage. Covers employee count, payroll, job classifications, and claims history.

Download PDF Request Help
