Common Local Questions

While not all areas in Surry County are high-risk, localized flooding can happen anywhere. Standard homeowners policies do not cover flood damage. We recommend checking your specific flood zone, especially near the Yadkin River or Ararat River basins.
Insurance rates can vary by zip code based on factors like traffic density, crime rates, and weather risks. However, the difference between Wilkesboro and Elkin is typically minimal. Your personal driving record and credit history have a much larger impact than the county line.
